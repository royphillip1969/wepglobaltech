About WEP Global Tech

WEP Global Tech is a systems engineering and cloud infrastructure consultancy founded by Elroy Phillip. We specialize in designing, deploying, and securing broadcast and cloud-based environments. Our mission is to empower organizations by integrating modern technologies with real-world reliability.

From broadcast control rooms to AWS cloud ecosystems, WEP Global Tech delivers end-to-end solutions that optimize performance, reduce downtime, and ensure operational security. We believe that technology should work for you — not the other way around.
