About WEP Global Tech

WEP Global Tech was founded by Elroy Phillip, a Broadcast Systems Administrator and Cloud Infrastructure Consultant with a deep background in media technology and IT operations. With expertise across broadcast engineering, network design, and cloud systems, WEP Global Tech bridges the gap between traditional infrastructure and modern cloud-driven environments.

Our mission is simple: Empowering Modern Systems Through Technology. We help organizations transition securely into cloud and hybrid models, optimize performance, and build reliable digital workflows. From AWS cloud deployments to broadcast control room automation, we bring hands-on expertise and innovation to every project.

WEP Global Tech also develops in-house solutions such as the R.A.T. (Report & Transmit) security reporting platform and the Resident-Verifier data importer for property management — reflecting our commitment to secure, practical, and purpose-built technology.
